## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I reset my streak?**
Streaks reset automatically if you miss a day. Unfortunately they cannot be manually restored.

**How often are new questions added?**
We add new questions regularly. Make sure you have auto-updates enabled to always get the latest content.

**How do I report a wrong answer?**
Email us at support@pakkafilmi.app with the question and we'll review it.

**What are the themed packs?**
Themed packs include Harry Potter, Marvel, Game of Thrones, Disney & Pixar, and 90s Nostalgia — with more coming soon.
