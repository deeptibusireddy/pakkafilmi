# Pakka Filmi — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026

## What we collect

Pakka Filmi stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Your XP score and current level
- Your daily streak count and last played date
- Your notification preferences
- Which daily challenges you have completed

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this data on our servers.**
There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, and no logins.
